ABOUT THE COMPANY

Due to the lack of availability of study material,we the students of engineering decided to create a platform where we could create an easy availabity of notes of every topic
There are a lot of applications made for school students and other sections of society but college students are provided platforms with the general content and hence they are wasting time on searching you tube channels and websites online.
We are inducing the sense of understanding with the affordability and ease of reach to the material.
MORE WE THOUGHT, MORE WE GREW, MORE WE BECAME
The idea just got refined and we came up as SRAZERS LLP, an educational company providing the soft notes and video lectures of every branch.

OUR MISSION

SRAZERS aims to provide the college students with a new platform which will help them to continue to their path of success but with smart work, better and fun learning.
We intend to create the education a fun learn process instead of a boring responsibility on their heads.

UNIQUENESS OF SRAZERS LLP

The uniqueness of the idea is to create college-specific content for the students of all branches.
