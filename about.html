About The Start-up

SRAZERS aims to provide the college students will a new platform which will help them to continue to their path of success but with smart work, better and fun learning.
This idea has its aim to help the students with their daily routines of studies.
We intend to create the education a fun learn process instead of a boring responsibility on their heads.
The uniqueness of the idea is to create college-specific content for the students.
Originality comes from the idea of regularly updating the application with revision sheets, tests, and new content and all of the things coming in one single application.
There are a lot of applications made for school students and other sections of society but college students are provided platforms with the general content and hence they are wasting time on searching you tube channels and websites online.
We are inducing the sense of understanding with the affordability and ease of reach to the material.
